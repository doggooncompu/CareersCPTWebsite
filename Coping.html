Coping Strategies

Whatis healthy coping strategy that you will help maintain a life/school/work balance in your future career? Explain it.

One coping strategy I will use is to spend time with friends. I will schedule certain times to set aside for my friends, especially in stressful times. Talking to others will allow you to release built-up stress and allow you to take a break from the stressful environment of engineering. For example, spending time with your friends after a stressful exam will help lower your stress and allow you to feel more refreshed and motivated for the next exam. A study conducted on the impact of friends on engineering students found that those with friends consistently have better mental health. In addition, friends can also help you when you are at rock bottom. Many friends have helped me push through difficult times and helped me through many difficult times, and by spending time with them in this pathway, I can push through things that I would not have been able to do on my own. In conclusion, the coping strategy I will use is spending time with friends as it allows you to be motivated and refreshed for your next exam and offers you support.
