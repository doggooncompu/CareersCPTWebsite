Career of Interest

What is my Career of Interest? Why is it a good fit for me?

My Career of Interest is an Aerospace engineer, specializing in the astronautical discipline. Aerospace engineers design, test, and develop aircraft, spacecraft, or any other related equipment. They ensure that the vehicle is functional and able to operate effectively. Such vehicles can include: Airliners, rockets, satellites and much more. Astronautical engineers specialize in spacecraft. Some examples include: rockets, satellites, Space shuttles and much more.

I think aerospace engineering is a great career choice for me because of my interest type, personality type, my top intelligence, and my passion for aerospace. My interest type is The Engineer. This interest type is investigative. Engineer-type interests tend to analyze problems using facts, logic, and research. This is incredibly important to Aerospace engineers because it allows them to design functional vehicles. Aerospace engineers require great analytical skills to identify problems with a given design and ways to achieve its capabilities, ensuring that the vehicle is safe and capable. For example, if the airplane design keeps failing to fly, aerospace engineers rely on analytical thinking and investigative skills to identify the problems in the airplane and find a solution to fix it. In addition, my personality type also makes me very well-suited as an aerospace engineer. My personality type is an ISTP, or the Crafter. According to the Myers-Briggs Foundation, ISTP seeks internal consistency and logic and tends to be analytical. Such a trait is incredibly important to aerospace engineers, as they must use logical reasoning and analytical skills to perfect certain designs. For example, an airplane might not fly as fast as we need it to. The only way an engineer can improve and optimize the design is through tedious amounts of analysis, which is a strong point of my personality. Additionally, my top intelligence communicates similar things. My top intelligence is logical/mathematical. This will definitely benefit me as an engineer due to the same reasons listed above. I will be able to use my logical and mathematical thinking skills, along with my analytical tendencies to perfect designs. Finally, I have always been passionate about Aerospace ever since I was young. I have always been inspired by stories from NASA, rockets, airplanes, and spacecraft. It has always inspired me to build and create, and it still inspires me to this day. It had consumed me in my childhood so completely that I still fold paper airplanes with scrap paper to this day. In conclusion, I am a great fit for aerospace engineers because of my beneficial interest type, personality type, my top intelligence, as well as my passion for aerospace engineering.
